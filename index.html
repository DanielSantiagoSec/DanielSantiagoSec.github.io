About

The Pivot

I spent over 10 years inside the operations engine of a Fortune 5 company — running logistics, leading incident response for enterprise "war rooms," and hardening access controls across 500+ operational roles long before I had a formal title for it. Somewhere in there I realized I wasn't just running operations, I was securing them.

That realization turned into a B.S. in Cybersecurity (3.91 GPA, Summa Cum Laude) with a Data Analytics concentration, a CompTIA Security+ certification, and a growing stack of hands-on IAM and cloud security work. I'm now channeling a decade of operational discipline into identity management, access governance, and Zero Trust design.

Off the Clock

I'm a new dad, and most of my "why" these days comes from watching my daughter experience things for the first time. When I'm not deep in a security rabbit hole (my wife has instituted the occasional "no more cybersecurity talk this week" rule), you'll find me chasing new towns, new food, and new experiences.

I co-founded Lehigh Valley Rent-a-Chef, used to compete with a powerlifting group, snowboard, and ride motorcycle. I also spent time doing equine therapy with my own horse, Maverick, working with clients facing neurological deficiencies.
